Uniform
---------

The Uniform distribution has the probability density function:

.. math::

    f(x;a,b)=
    \begin{cases}
    \frac{1}{b-a}\,, &  x \in [a,b]\,, \\
    0, & \text{otherwise,}
    \end{cases}
    
where :math:`b` is the **upper bound** and :math:`a<b` the **lower bound**.
